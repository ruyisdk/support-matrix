### Hardware Information

- Sipeed LicheePi RV Dock
- A USB-A Power Adapter

- A microSD Card
- A USB to UART Debugger (e.g., CH340, CH341, FT2232, etc.)
- Three DuPont Wires

Use `dd` to flash the image to the microSD card.
